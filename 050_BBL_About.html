KALI ELLISON

Kali Ellison is the founder, CEO, accountant, salesperson, advertiser, asset manager, software developer, and janitor of Bell Bag Loans.
He wears all the hats to make it easy for people to rid themselves of debt.

Freedom is my highest value.
Debt is my tightest shackle.
Freedom of education got me a Bachelors of Science in Mechanical Engineering from The Ohio State University.
Debt got me $300,000 in student loans.
I know the pain and stress that comes from being loaded with debt.
That's why my mission is to eliminate that pain and stress for the people of California.

I've read books and papers, done the research, filed forms, drafted contracts, gathered funds, built a website, and much more.
I've jumped through many hoops to get here, but all of that effort means nothing without you.
I need your help
Together, we can eliminate debt.

I was inspired to start Bell Bag Loans by a certain raccoon who issues loans with no interest and no time limit on repayment.
I believe the people of Califronia are responsible enough to handle this freedom, if they are given the chance.
With your help, we can give the people of California that chance by freeing them from debt
We can build a world with happier people and a stonger economy.
