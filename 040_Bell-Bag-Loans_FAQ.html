Q: What if I don't have access to Zelle?

We can walk you through opening an account and setting up payments that does use Zelle.

If not, that's okay. We accept other forms of payment.

Venmo, CashApp, PayPal, Checks, MoneyOrders, and Cash

If you have another preferred method, feel free to ask about it.

Q: Can I pay with a credit card?

We do not accept credit cards. The goal is to get you out of debt, not to move it somewhere else.

Q: Why don't you just forgive my debt?

We can do that, but there are two reasons we don't.

1) You will have to pay taxes on any amount that is forgiven.

2) Your payments finance debt relief for others.
